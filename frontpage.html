The Ringlight client keeps track of changes to your collection of files and manages transferring those files peer-to-peer to other computers. For more information about the Ringlight client, check out the learn more page or visit the discussion group.


Ringlight Client Features

  • Compact - It's fast to download and installs quickly.
  • Lightweight - You'll never even notice it's running.
  • Instant Sharing - Add a file to your shares directory, and it's online. Never wait to upload.


Don't worry, no files will be shared until you set your privacy settings.
