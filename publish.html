Publish

Ringlight allows you to publish files, as well as share them privately

There are many advantages to using Ringlight to put your files on the web:

  • Instant sharing - never wait on an upload again
  • No limit on file size
  • No limit on the number of files - access ALL your files!
  • Automatic Thumbnails - thumbnails are automatically generated for photos and videos, just like on your computer!
  • Search Engine Optimization - your public files will be registered with search engines, to maximize hits
