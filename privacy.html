General

When you register for an account, we ask you for some information, including an optional e-mail address. You can provide additional information when you edit profiles for your account, machines, folders, or files. We track some of your interactions with the site, such as what pages are viewed and what files are downloaded. Our servers automatically receive information from your browser, including the URL of the page being requested, your computer's IP address, and cookies stored in your browser. Your browser sends this same information to our third-party providers of website analytics and advertising.

Children

This site is not designed for individuals under 18. If a parent or guardian believes that their child has provided us personally identifiable information without their consent then they should contact us at brandon@ringlight.us and we will remove it. We do not collect personally indentifiable information from anyone under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with such information, then we will delete it.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not rent, sell, or give your information with anyone else, with the following exceptions: We will respond to subpeonas or other court orders and will defend against legal claims against us. We will cooperate with law enforcement in order to prevent or take action against illegal activities, suspected fraud, threats to the safety of a person, violation of the terms of service, or as otherwise required by law. In the event that we are acquired by another company, they will obtain access to your information. In this case, we will inform you if this change of ownership will affect the privacy policy.

Cookies

We set and access cookies on your computer.

Logs

We log various information related to site access and usage. We use this information for technical administration of the site, such as identifying problems in service.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. If we do then we will mention it in on the Ringlight news blog.
