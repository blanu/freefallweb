What is Ringlight?

Ringlight shares files from your computer to your world.

Ringlight is a way to access your files from anywhere and to share those files with anyone, simply and easily. Since basic accounts are free, you can experience the mobility and freedom that Ringlight offers right now.

  • Access

    Access your files from anywhere that has a web browser, even when your computer is off!

  • Organize

    Tag your files, add metadata, and search through all your files on all your computers.

  • Share

    Share files easily with friends, family, coworkers, and clients. There's no limit on file size!

  • Publish

    Publish your files to the world on your own free website, which is automatically kept up-to-date when you add new files.
