Share

Sharing your files with friends, family, coworkers, and clients has never been easier

Ringlight offers several ways to share your files:

  • E-mail - send a link via e-mail right from the website, and grant access at the same time
  • Ringlight users - if they have a Ringlight account, just type in their username, with autocomplete!
  • Twitter - share a link on Twitter with a note, right from the website!
  • Everything else - Every file has a URL, so you can send it via IM, link to it from another website, whatever works for you
